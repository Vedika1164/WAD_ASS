PICT Clubs & Activities

Coding Club

Description: Encourages problem-solving, competitive programming, and hackathons.

Achievements: Winners at Smart India Hackathon, CodeChef 5-star coders.

Robotics Club

Description: Builds innovative robots for competitions and real-life challenges.

Achievements: Finalists in Robocon, National Robotics Championship winners.

Dance Club

Description: Performs classical, western, and fusion dance styles in college fests.

Achievements: First prize at MIT Summit, performed at Mood Indigo (IIT Bombay).

Drama Club

Description: Performs street plays, skits, and stage dramas on social issues.

Achievements: Winners at Firodiya Karandak and Purushottam Karandak.

PICT NSS

Description: Engages in social work, rural development, and awareness programs.

Achievements: Tree plantation drives, blood donation camps, Swachh Bharat events.
